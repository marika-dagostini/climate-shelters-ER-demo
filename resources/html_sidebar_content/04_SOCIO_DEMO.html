This section presents a set of indicators describing the size and composition of the population, including both demographic and socio-economic characteristics, based on the 2021 Permanent Population Census.

Each indicator is grouped into population deciles (each decile contains one-tenth of the total population of Emilia-Romagna).
  • Population: total number of residents
  • Socio-economic Deprivation Index: combined measure based on low educational attainment, lack of participation in the labor market, and housing overcrowding, based on 2021 data
  • Elderly: percentage of people aged 75 and over relative to the total population
  • Children: percentage of children aged 0-4 relative to the total population
  • Foreign Residents: percentage of people with non-Italian citizenship relative to the total population
  • People Leaving Alone: percentage of one-person households relative to the total population
The Socio-economic Deprivation Index ( ID 21 ) is calculated as the combination of three indicators:
  • X1: percentage of people aged 20+ with an education level below upper secondary school
  • X2: percentage of people aged 15-64 who are not employed
  • X3: average number of residents per dwelling unit
